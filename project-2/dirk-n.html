Dirk Nowitzki

Dirk Nowitzki is a German national basketball player that played 21 seasons in the National Basketball League for the Dallas Mavericks. Listed as 7ft 0in, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest Europen basketball player of all time. Dirk Nowitzki. Known for his scoring ability, versatility, accurate outside shooting, and trademark fadeaway jump shot Dirk has won a barage of achievements under his belt, including the 2011 NBA finals where a 32 year old Nowitzki took down the Miami Heat's big three in prime Lebron James, Dwayne Wade, and Chris Bosh. In 2021 Nowitzki was inducted into the Naismith Hall of Fame and will continue to leave a ever last impact on the game of basketball.
